ABOUT US

Aliarpur Aziz High School is a public school located in Badarganj Bazar, Chuadanga Sadar, Bangladesh. Since its establishment in 1966, Aliarpur Aziz High School served the motherland in many ways. Aliarpur Aziz High School is noted for its academic performance and extracurricular activities, having produced many notable alumni including leaders in business, the military and state and national politics as well as senior government officers, scientists and engineers. The curriculum of Aliarpur Aziz High School includes secondary level academic subjects from the grade 6th to 10th respectively. The curriculum for secondary level academic subjects is introduced by the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Jessore, Bangladesh. Students of the secondary level have to elect one of the three major programs: Art s and Humanities; Commerce; and Science. Students have some compulsory subjects and some optional subjects in each of the programs.
